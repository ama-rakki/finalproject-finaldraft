Featured Products

This gallery includes some items I use everyday to spice up my notebooks, journals, and other papers. Each of these products are standouts in my stationary collection and I recommend them as staples in any desk setup! To start, my pen collection is never complete without the signature Muji Pen (in 0.38 width of course). But the Muji Pencil (0.5 mm) is another gem often overlooked. My most expensive pencil is the Kuru Toga, which I was convinced into owning due to their adorable Sanrio collab. And while Copic markers are often used by illustrators and comic artists, it also works great as a highlighter!

Items in the washi tape gallery are all from the ZDOLAKIDS collection as featured in my product reviews page! My favorite colors to use are pink, green, and a neutral black. But all colors of the rainbow are included in this collection, which makes for lots of fun decorating choices!

My sticker collection is a prized monument on my desk, and each sheet is accumulated and taped into a blank journal (which I refer to as the mega sticker book catalog!) Buying individual sticker sheets is a dandy indulgence but investing in a larger sticker book (like the featured San-X book) is a great way to get multiple pages of stickers for a better price.
